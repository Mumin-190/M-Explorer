# M-Explorer Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** Beta-3 Release

M-Explorer is built from the ground up to respect your privacy and data security. 

## No Telemetry or Analytics
M-Explorer does **not** contain any telemetry, analytics tracking, or crash-reporting frameworks. We do not track your usage, screen time, or feature interactions.

## No Account Requirements
You do not need to create an account, log in, or provide an email address to use this application. The app operates completely anonymously.

## Local File Processing
All file operations, rendering, indexing, and thumbnail generations are performed 100% locally on your device hardware. M-Explorer does not upload your files, photos, videos, or documents to any external servers or third-party APIs.

## Network Functionality
M-Explorer requests Network and Internet permissions strictly to facilitate **your explicitly configured** connections:
- Connecting to your private FTP, SFTP, SMB, or WebDAV servers.
- Hosting a local SFTP server on your device for wireless transfers on your local Wi-Fi.

M-Explorer will never initiate outbound network connections to external services outside of the remotes you manually configure. 

## Permissions
If you grant "All Files Access" or "Shizuku/Root" permissions, those privileges are strictly confined to the application sandbox for your own direct file management tasks.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy statement or the application's source code, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
